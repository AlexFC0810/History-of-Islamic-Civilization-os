# Thread Radar

## Purpose

This file is the steward’s radar: the trails it should keep watching, researching, and surfacing.

The agent should not wait for perfect prompts. It should notice when a thread deserves attention.

## Prime Question

> **What civilizational pattern is hiding in plain sight?**

## Active Thread Families

### 1. Prophetic seed-code

Questions:

- What early principles later scaled into institutions?
- What did Mecca form that Medina could operationalize?
- How did mercy become architecture?
- How did wealth become trust?
- How did knowledge become sacred duty?

### 2. Islamic Golden Ages plural

Questions:

- Which regions are missing from the map?
- Which eras are wrongly excluded?
- How should Ottoman, Mughal, Safavid, Mamluk, Timurid, West African, Andalusian, and Southeast Asian arcs be classified?
- What makes an age “golden” beyond science alone?

### 3. Decline and renewal

Questions:

- Which loops broke?
- Which loops were attacked?
- Which loops failed to adapt?
- Which loops can be rebuilt first?
- What does sovereignty mean in a world of finance, platforms, AI, media, and military dependency?

### 4. Finance, riba, and moral capital

Questions:

- What would substance-first Islamic finance look like?
- What would a modern waqf lab do?
- How can diaspora capital become public-benefit infrastructure?
- What can be funded now that would still matter 100 years from now?

### 5. Women and civilizational flourishing

Questions:

- Which women scholars, patrons, transmitters, founders, healers, teachers, and builders are missing from public memory?
- How do we avoid reducing women’s role to one or two examples?
- How did family strength and women’s learning interact historically?

### 6. Comparative civilizational memory

Questions:

- What did Islamic civilization do better than modern systems?
- What did modern systems solve that Muslim societies need to learn from?
- Where are the false binaries: Islam vs West, faith vs reason, medieval vs ignorant, tradition vs progress?

### 7. Knowledge integrity

Questions:

- What can isnad teach modern AI and information integrity?
- What can adab teach debate culture?
- What can source hardening teach viral media?
- What would a citation-native public education system look like?

### 8. Modern Digital House of Wisdom

Questions:

- What would a 21st-century Bayt al-Hikmah be?
- Is it a media studio, research lab, translation engine, AI knowledge graph, public library, waqf, or all of the above?
- What is the minimum viable institution?

## Thread Triage

When a new thread appears, classify it:

- **Canon:** belongs in durable repo files.
- **Source ledger:** promising but needs evidence.
- **Content:** ready for public explanation.
- **Visual:** needs a diagram or map.
- **Debate bank:** useful response to a common objection.
- **Implementation:** can become a real institution, product, or fund.

## Final Radar Line

> **Follow the thread until it becomes a map. Then preserve the map.**
