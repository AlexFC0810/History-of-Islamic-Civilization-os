# Calibration Meta Facts

High-impact historical recalibration facts for content systems.

## Tier 1

### Animal Welfare
Islamic civilization developed explicit animal welfare norms centuries before modern European animal welfare legislation.

Public framing:
"Most people assume animal welfare is a modern moral invention. History is more interesting than that."

### Rules of War
Historical Islamic legal traditions developed explicit wartime constraints during eras when unrestricted warfare was common.

Public framing:
"The existence of war ethics is historically more significant when compared to the baseline norms of the era."

### Christian Bridge Fact
Jesus is mentioned extensively in the Qur'an, and Mary holds uniquely elevated status in Islamic scripture.

Public framing:
"Many Christians have never been taught how much reverence Islam has for Jesus and Mary."
