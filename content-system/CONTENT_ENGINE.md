# Content Engine

## Core Strategy
Do not lead with doctrinal argument.
Lead with curiosity, calibration, overlooked history, and moral surprise.

## Primary Series

### What School Didn't Teach You
Format:
Historical recalibration.

Examples:
- Animal welfare before modernity.
- Medieval hygiene realities.
- Preservation of knowledge.
- Rules of war history.

---

### Civilization Stress Tests
Question:
How does a civilization behave under pressure?

Dimensions:
- war
- famine
- dissent
- wealth
- power
- minority treatment

---

### Compassion Without Witness
Stories of ethics where no applause exists.

Examples:
- animals
- charity
- treatment of captives

---

### Jesus Bridge
Christian-calibrated content.

Questions:
- What does Islam actually say about Jesus?
- Why is Mary so honored?
- What would surprise most Christians?

---

### Empire vs Revelation
Historical systems analysis.

Distinguish:
- prophetic teachings
- institutional religion
- empire behavior

---

## Content Rules

Never:
- humiliate viewers
- lead with superiority claims
- trigger tribal defensiveness unnecessarily

Prefer:
- surprise
- curiosity
- asymmetry
- honest nuance
