## Master Thesis

> **The Prophetic age was the seed operating system: the period where revelation transformed the soul, disciplined power, moralized wealth, organized community, sanctified knowledge, and turned mercy into social architecture.**

## Why this matters

The Islamic Golden Ages cannot be understood only as later scientific, artistic, medical, or institutional achievement. They must be studied as historical fruit from earlier seed-code:

- tawhid and moral accountability;
- purification of intention;
- worship as formation;
- knowledge as duty;
- wealth as trust;
- law as welfare;
- markets under ethics;
- community beyond tribe;
- mercy toward the vulnerable;
- power under accountability.
